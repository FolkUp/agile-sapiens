# AI Transparency Statement

**Required by:** EU AI Act Article 50
**Effective Date:** March 26, 2026
**Publisher:** FolkUp Ecosystem

## Automated Tool Usage Disclosure

In compliance with **Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act), Article 50**, we transparently disclose that **automated tools have been used** in the development of content for the AGILE SAPIENS monograph.

## Scope of Automated Tool Usage

Automated tools are utilized for the following purposes:

### ✅ **Research and Analysis Support**
- Information gathering and preliminary research
- Source identification and fact-checking assistance
- Data analysis and pattern recognition

### ✅ **Content Development Assistance**
- Editorial drafting support
- Style and voice consistency maintenance
- Content structuring and organization

### ✅ **Quality Assurance Support**
- Grammar and language optimization
- Fact verification assistance
- Citation and reference formatting

## Human Oversight Guarantee

### 🛡️ **Mandatory Human Review**
**Every piece of published content** undergoes comprehensive human editorial review by qualified professionals before publication.

### 🛡️ **No Automated Publication**
**Zero automated publication:** No content is published without human oversight, fact-checking, and explicit approval.

### 🛡️ **Editorial Accountability**
All published content includes:
- `reviewed_by`: Name of human editor responsible
- `review_date`: Date of editorial review completion
- `sources`: Verified source references

## Quality Standards

### Banking-Level Accuracy
- All factual claims verified through multiple independent sources
- Scientific rigor maintained throughout all content
- Professional editorial standards applied consistently

### Voice and Authenticity
- "Harari meets Dilbert" voice archetype maintained by human editors
- Authentic scientific popular writing style preserved
- Original analysis and insights developed through human expertise

## Transparency Commitment

We commit to:
- **Clear labeling** of automated tool usage where applicable
- **Continuous disclosure** of our editorial processes
- **Regular updates** to this transparency statement as processes evolve
- **Responsive communication** regarding our methodology
