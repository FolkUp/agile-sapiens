- **Supervisory Authority:** Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD) - Portugal

## 2. Legal Basis

This privacy policy is implemented in accordance with:
- Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation - GDPR)
- Lei n.º 58/2019 (Portuguese GDPR implementation law)
- Directive 2002/58/EC (ePrivacy Directive)

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our site, we may automatically collect:
- IP address (anonymized)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring website
- Pages visited and time spent
- Date and time of access

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) for website functionality and security.

### 3.2 Information You Provide
- Email address (if you contact us)
- Name (if you provide it voluntarily)
- Any content you submit through contact forms

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) or legitimate interests for responding to inquiries.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Site
- Improve user experience
- Respond to your inquiries
- Ensure site security
- Comply with legal obligations

**Retention Period:** Server logs are retained for 30 days maximum. Contact information is retained for 2 years or until you request deletion.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only:
- With hosting providers (Hetzner Online GmbH, Germany - GDPR compliant)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or safety

**Data Transfers:** Any data transfers outside the EU are protected by appropriate safeguards under GDPR Chapter V.

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data (Art. 15)
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") (Art. 17)
- **Restrict processing** (Art. 18)
- **Data portability** (Art. 20)
- **Object** to processing (Art. 21)
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (Art. 7)

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@folkup.app

**Response Time:** We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR Art. 12.
