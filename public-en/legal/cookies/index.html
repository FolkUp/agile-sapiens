    2. What Are Cookies

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide functionality.

    4. Cookie Consent

    4.1 Your Choices

    • Essential cookies: Cannot be disabled as they’re necessary for site functionality
    • Non-essential cookies: Require your explicit consent
    • Third-party widgets: Blocked until you give consent

    4.2 Managing Consent

    You can:

    • Change your cookie preferences using our consent banner
    • Block cookies through your browser settings
    • Withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies or updating preferences

    4.3 Browser Settings

    Most browsers allow you to:

    • Block all cookies
    • Block third-party cookies only
    • Clear existing cookies
    • Receive notifications before cookies are set

    For browser-specific instructions:

    • Chrome
    • Firefox
    • Safari
    • Edge

    5. Impact of Blocking Cookies

    Essential Cookies

    Blocking these may result in:

    • Loss of theme preferences
    • Repeated cookie consent prompts
    • Reduced functionality

    Non-Essential Cookies

    Blocking these will not affect core site functionality.

    6. Data Retention

    • Cookie consent: 1 year or until withdrawn
    • Theme preferences: 1 year or until cleared
    • Session data: Cleared when browser is closed

    7. International Transfers

    Ko-fi services may involve data transfers outside the EU. Ko-fi implements appropriate safeguards under GDPR Chapter V.

    8. Your Rights

    Under GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data processed through cookies:

    • Access your data (Art. 15)
    • Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
    • Erasure (“right to be forgotten”) (Art. 17)
    • Restrict processing (Art. 18)
    • Data portability (Art. 20)
    • Object to processing (Art. 21)
    • Withdraw consent (Art. 7)

    Contact us at privacy@folkup.app to exercise these rights.

    9. Children’s Privacy

    Our site is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through cookies.
